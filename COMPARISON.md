## What Makes Mauxx AI Unique

1. **Skill-first architecture** — Uses Claude's native skills, doesn't replace them
2. **Model tiering** — Haiku/Sonnet/Opus routing saves 85%+ on costs
3. **Free-tier stack** — Production infrastructure for $0
4. **Tool accuracy** — Self-improving as it proves itself
5. **Persistent memory** — File-based, portable, vector-searchable
6. **Neon TUI** — Beautiful, terminal-native interface
7. **Transparent** — Every decision on disk, full audit trail
8. **Lightweight** — ~50KB of config + skills, runs anywhere
